Core Beliefs of Islam

Islam is based on six core beliefs known as the "Articles of Faith":

Belief in Allah

Belief in the oneness of God (Tawhid) and that Muhammad (PBUH) is His final messenger.

Belief in Angels

Belief in the existence of angels created by Allah to perform various duties.

Belief in Holy Books

Belief in the divine scriptures including the Quran, Torah, Psalms, and Gospel.

Belief in Prophets

Belief in all prophets from Adam to Noah, Abraham, Moses, Jesus, and Muhammad (peace be upon them all).

Belief in the Day of Judgment

Belief in resurrection and accountability for one's deeds in this life.

Belief in Divine Decree

Belief that everything happens according to Allah's will and knowledge.
